How Three-Way Matching Works

Three-way matching compares three documents to ensure accuracy:

  1. Purchase Order (PO) - What was ordered and at what price
  2. Goods Receipt Note (GRN) - What was actually received
  3. Vendor Invoice - What the vendor is charging

The system checks:

  • Quantity Match: GRN received qty vs PO ordered qty
  • Price Match: Invoice total vs PO total
